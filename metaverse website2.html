Smart city

Arduino-based innovation for a sustainable future

About the Project

This Smart City model demonstrates how technology can solve real-world problems using Arduino systems. The project integrates multiple systems to ensure like Waste to electricity or WTE, Air quality monitoring system, Flood alarm system, Earthquake detction system, Smart irrigation sustainability, safety, and efficiency in urban areas.

Features

1. Waste-to-Electricity Generator

Converts waste like plastic paper into usable energy, reducing pollution and providing clean power which can use in powering villages.

2. Air Quality Monitoring

Continuously measures air pollution levels and helps in keeping track of environmental health.

3. Flood Alarm System

Detects rising water levels and produce sound as a indication of alert to prevent damage and ensure safety.

4. Earthquake Detection System

Uses vibration sensors to detect the vibration and warn people in advance by producing sound.

5. Smart Irrigation

Automatically switches the pump ON when soil is dry and OFF when it is moist, saving water and labor.

Future Scope

This model can be expanded with IoT-based dashboards, AI prediction systems, and integration with smart grids to make cities more resilient and sustainable.
